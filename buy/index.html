Buying an account
Yes, you can register a 10 day SBProX demo account directly from the platform. Download the platform, run SBProX, click the "Demo access" button, enter your email and click "Register". After some time, a letter with access data from a 10-day demo account will be sent to the specified email. Demo registration is possible only once. It is not possible to re-register a demo.
Choose the appropriate tariff on the "Tariffs" page, click "Buy", in the payment form, be sure to indicate the email address to which the SBProX account will be registered. Immediately after successful payment, an email with a login and password, as well as links to instructions for connecting and setting up the platform, will automatically be sent to the specified email.
First check the Spam folder in your mailbox, sometimes emails end up there for some reason. If you have paid for the platform license and have not received an email with access data, please contact us in any of the ways. Do not forget to indicate the time of payment, the email specified during the purchase, and the details from which the payment was made (card number and bank statement on a successful transaction).
Unfortunately, according to paragraph 1 of the "User Agreement", the money for paying for the account is not returned. You can register a 10 day demo account and test the platform beforehand. Also, it is necessary that your computer meets the "System Requirements" for the correct operation of the platform.
